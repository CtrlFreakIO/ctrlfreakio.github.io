**Type:** Research project
**Tools:** Python, NetworkX, EN ISO 13849-1

Developed a graph-based numerical method for preliminary safety category assessment of control systems. The method converts electrical schematics into directed graphs and applies a numerical scoring algorithm to estimate the achievable safety category under EN ISO 13849-1 — providing engineers with early feedback before a full SISTEMA analysis is performed.

The work addresses a practical gap in the safety engineering workflow: formal tools like SISTEMA require complete documentation that is rarely available in early design stages. The proposed method allows preliminary assessment from partial information, helping to identify design issues earlier in the process.

**Key aspects:**
- Directed graph representation of safety-related electrical schematics
- Numerical scoring aligned with EN ISO 13849-1 category requirements
- Python implementation using NetworkX
- Validated against SISTEMA reference cases
